Starting Company/TYE

Earlier last year I applied to an entreupreneurship program TYE , where we learn about what it takes to start a business and then make one ourselves. A couple of friends and I in the program met up and decided to start our company, Achilles Heal. The name is from Achilles' heel, the Greek story about the warrior. The company is going to make a better way for trainers to communicate to kids that were injured in their sport. We are making an index for the trainer to refer to while training the student to make the process pleasant for both the trainer and the athelete. This is a great learning experience for me, as I would like to start my own company when I'm older.
