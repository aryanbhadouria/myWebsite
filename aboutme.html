Who am I?

Hi! I'm Aryan Bhadouria. I'm a sophomore and class treasurer at Mount Saint Charles. I made this website myself for fun with HTML, CSS, and Javascript with BIG help from w3schools. I really like technology, dogs (even though I don't have any), and being with people. This is my world! I'm in the process of figuring out my interests right now. I've narrowed them down to technology and robotics, but I'm still working at it. I've done and participated in a lot of stuff in this process, such as varsity indoor and outdoor track, boyscouts, playing the saxophone, playing the guitar, coding, clubs, etc.. Throughout my life, I've noticed 3 things that are the most vital to me: first, that before academics and sports, I should focus on myself and how I make others feel more, second, the importance of math and science in my lives, and third, learning from all of my mistakes and becoming a better person instead of sullying over them.
